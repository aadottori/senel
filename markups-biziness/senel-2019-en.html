ABOUT US

UFRJ's Electric Engineering Week is an event held at the Tecnology Center of the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro, organized by the Eletric Engineering Department. The event has as a goal the capacitation of engineers and future engineers, aimed to the new tendencies of energy sectors, technology and job market, offering them a diversity of activities during the week.
